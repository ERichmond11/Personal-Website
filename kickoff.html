Kickoff Toronto

A platform and community initiative helping Toronto football talent get discovered, connected, and supported.

Partner With Me See Resume

What It Is

Kickoff Toronto is a structured showcase and content ecosystem focused on youth and emerging football players. The goal is simple: make local talent visible and easier for coaches, academies, and supporters to find.

My Role

Product & Platform

Defined user flows and content structure, and shipped web experiences that present player and event information clearly.

Operations

Coordinated event planning, communication workflows, and the participant experience from signup to follow-up.

Community & Growth

Built early audience momentum through social content, direct outreach, and local network partnerships.

Why This Project Matters

  • Access: Creates a visible path for players who might otherwise be overlooked.
  • Trust: Brings players, families, and coaches into one transparent experience.
  • Execution: Demonstrates my ability to lead both software delivery and real-world operations.

Tech + Workflow Highlights

Frontend UI Data Modeling Event Logistics Stakeholder Communication Analytics Tracking

Next Up

The roadmap includes better participant profiles, richer event reporting, and stronger coach-facing views to speed up talent evaluation.
