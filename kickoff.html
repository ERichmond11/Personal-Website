What It Is

Kickoff Toronto is an unofficial fan guide for visitors and locals planning their city experience around 2026 events in Toronto. It combines venue-adjacent recommendations, itinerary planning, and updates into one mobile experience.

My Role

Product Owner

Defined the core product experience and feature set for discovery, planning, and updates in a fan-first mobile workflow.

Technical Lead

Shipped version 1.0 for iOS, including account-based planning flows, data-driven content sections, and release execution.

Growth & Content

Structured curated places, team-following, and local event/news surfaces to improve relevance and repeat usage.

Core App Capabilities

  • Explore Toronto: Curated picks across dining, bars, hotels, nightlife, shopping, and attractions.
  • Build Itineraries: Create and save custom day plans with timing, ordering, and map-aware flow.
  • Follow Teams + News: Track favorite teams and get relevant match/news updates in one feed.
